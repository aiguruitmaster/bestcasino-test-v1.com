Register an account, verify your identity (KYC process), and make your first qualifying deposit. In Belgium, winnings from BGC-licensed casinos are generally not subject to income tax for individual players — the casino operator pays a gambling tax to the Belgian government. First, contact the casino's customer support team directly — most issues can be resolved through live chat. The minimum age to gamble at any licensed casino in Belgium — online or land-based — is 21 years old. This is stricter than many other EU countries which set the minimum at 18.
